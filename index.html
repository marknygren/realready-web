FAQ

Common questions

Does RealReady cover my state?

Yes. All 50 states plus Washington, D.C. You’ll get both the national principles and the rules specific to the jurisdiction you’re licensing in.

Is it really free to start?

Yes. You can practice on the website right away for free. The app is a one-time unlock for the full state-specific question bank, progress tracking, and missed-question mode — no subscriptions.

What topics are covered?

The national content is organized into nine focused categories, including Property & Land Use, Contracts, Agency, Finance, and Real Estate Math. State-specific rules are broken down by category inside each state’s content, so every topic is covered where it fits.

Is this the same as my pre-license course?

No. RealReady is exam prep. Use it after (or alongside) your required course to make sure you pass the state exam itself.

Does it work offline?

Yes. The app caches questions locally so you can study without a signal.

Do you guarantee I’ll pass?

No one can promise you a pass. But targeted practice and honest feedback on what you’re missing is the closest thing.
